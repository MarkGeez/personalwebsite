Machu Picchu

Historic Sanctuary of Machu Picchu


Why I want to visit this beautiful place: I’ve always been curious about ancient civilizations, so visiting Machu Picchu would be incredible. Hiking through the Andes and finally seeing the ruins sounds like an unforgettable experience. It’s amazing how it was built without modern tools and still stands today. Sitting on one of the terraces and taking in the view would be so peaceful. Watching the sunrise over the ruins would be breathtaking. It’s one of those places that feels like a must-see in life.


Details about this beautiful Place: Machu Picchu is located in the Andes Mountains of Peru and was built by the Inca Empire in the 15th century. It’s about 2,430 meters above sea level and is known for its dry-stone construction and terraced fields. It was rediscovered by Hiram Bingham in 1911 and is now a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
