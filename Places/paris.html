Eiffel Tower

One of France's Engineering Marvel


Why I want to visit this beautiful place: I’ve always wanted to see the Eiffel Tower because it’s such a famous landmark. The thought of standing under it at night, with the love of my life in the city of love with the lights sparkling sounds amazing. I’d love to have a picnic at Champ de Mars and take some photos with the tower in the background. Climbing to the top and seeing the whole city sounds exciting. It would feel like stepping into a romantic movie.


Details about this beautiful place: The Eiffel Tower is located in Paris, France, and was designed by Gustave Eiffel. It was completed in 1889 for the Exposition Universelle (World's Fair) and stands at 324 meters tall. It’s one of the most visited paid monuments in the world, with millions of visitors each year.
